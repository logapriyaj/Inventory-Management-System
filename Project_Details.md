Project Title
Inventory Management System
Problem Statement
Many small and medium-sized businesses still manage inventory manually using paper records or spreadsheets. This often leads to stock mismanagement, inaccurate inventory records, product shortages, overstocking, and increased operational costs. There is a need for a centralized Inventory Management System that can efficiently track products, monitor stock levels, manage suppliers, record sales and purchases, and generate reports to improve business operations and decision-making.
Project Objectives
To develop a system for managing inventory records digitally.
To maintain accurate information about products and stock levels.
To automate inventory updates during purchases and sales.
To manage supplier information efficiently.
To generate inventory, sales, and purchase reports.
To reduce manual errors in stock management.
To provide secure login and access control for users.
To improve overall inventory tracking and business productivity.
